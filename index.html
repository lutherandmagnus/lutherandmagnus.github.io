Dead Man’s Lode

In development — solo & co-op

The map is your weapon.

Dig kill-corridors and funnel the horde. Mine gold — and your own escape routes — out of the walls. Keep the lantern fed, because the things down there hear you digging. Then gather your crew, vote to descend, and pray you make it back up with the haul.

A co-op survivors-like set in a cursed gold rush, for 1–4 prospectors.

Follow the dig → itch.io devlog · @deadmanslode

The label

Luther & Magnus is named after two cats who supervise all development from on top of the keyboard. They contribute nothing and take full credit.

Behind them: one developer, building games solo with modern tools — including a healthy amount of AI assistance on the code — so that small, strange, big-hearted games can actually get finished.
